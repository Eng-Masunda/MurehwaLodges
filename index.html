Location:

Located deep in the heart of Mashonaland west, 65km away from the capital Harare in Zimbabwe, sub-Saharan Africa. Murehwa lodges is situated on a plateau overlooking a beautiful valley with the mighty Ganjga river snaking its way down the hills and through the valley. It is also located 10km away from the nearest hospital and township at Murehwa center. Visitors can drive in or be driven from the airport by our transport service.

Description:

Murehwa lodges is a beautiful property that spans 64 square kilometers. It is rich in wildlife and beautiful mountainous scenery.It has lodges and camp sites for camping. It also has swimming pools for each lodge, a jacuzzi in the 5-star Princton lodge, an entertainment center which offers wifi and tv , a cafeteria, medical center and a small gymn.

Wildlife:

Murehwa lodges has diverse wildlife species and is a favored destination for plains game hunters and wildlife enthusiasts. It is home to the herbivore big five of Africa, Elephant, Rhino, Giraffe, Buffalo and Eland. We have former hunters and park rangers making up our tour-guide team.The river Ganjga has a myriad of different fish and a fishing tournament is held annually between June and July.
